Weeks

A Course workflow is divided into weeks. By default, the workflow always contains one week. The week can be renamed to be given a title or to organize your course by topic/unit by double clicking the text.

Manipulating Weeks

Weeks can be manipulated in various ways:
  • Adding Weeks
    • To add a week below another, click the icon that appears when you hover over the week.
  • Deleting Weeks
    • A week can be deleted by clicking the icon that appears when you hover over it. All nodes contained within it will also be deleted.
  • Cloning Weeks
    • To duplicate a week, and all its contents, click the icon that appears when you hover over the week.
