Columns

Workflows are organized into columns into which the nodes are sorted. The exact columns depend on the type of workflow. Columns are added automatically to new workflows.

Manipulating Columns

  • Moving Columns
    • To move a column, click and drag the head of the column (the icon). This allows you to rearrange the columns to your liking.
  • Adding Columns
    • Sometimes a workflow can have more than just the default columns. When you insert a node of a type that does not currently have a column, a new column will be created automatically.
